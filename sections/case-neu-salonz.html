The Brief

NEU Salonz ran six branches across Gurgaon. We started as their agency. After one Valentine’s campaign, they poached us in-house as Marketing Head. Then we built campaigns that became legends — and templates other salon brands tried to replicate.

What We Did

“We treated salons like brands — not service businesses. Every campaign was a launch. Every artist was a creator. Every branch was a flagship.”

The Campaigns

01

Game of Tones

Hair colour campaign timed to a Game of Thrones season launch. Themed F&B (DothraTEA, HODORITOS), GOT soundtrack in-salon, iron throne photo cutout and Joffrey dartboard.

Outcome: Sold a year’s worth of hair colour in ~2 months · Nykaa later copied the name

02

South Point Mall 6th Anniversary

Member-exclusive event. Grand decor, premium F&B, curated goodies, a 7-foot selfie wall and hired photographers.

Outcome: Rs.21 Lakh in sales in 3 days

03

imNEU Membership

Repositioned the salon membership as a lifestyle membership. PVC cards in White, Silver, Gold and Black tiers, with cross-brand partnerships.

Outcome: 2x memberships in 30 days

04

NEUresolution

Annual purpose campaign — makeovers for underprivileged children and homeless transformation films.

Outcome: 500K+ views on Facebook · Positioned NEU as a brand with a soul

“After NEU, every major salon brand in Gurgaon came calling — Looks, Geetanjali, Marco Aldany, Headmasters, Dessange Paris, Toni & Guy. We didn’t pitch any of them. They came to us.”
