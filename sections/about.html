Where the name comes from

SKOLL

/skøl/ — Norse origin

Skoll comes from Norse mythology. He's the wolf who chases the sun across the sky: relentlessly, strategically, never closing the gap and never giving up.

The wolf represents intent. The sun represents the vision. The chase represents the work.
