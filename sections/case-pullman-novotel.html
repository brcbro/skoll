The Brief

A 5-star hotel group needed performance marketing for restaurant promotions and wedding lead generation. They’d worked with larger agencies. The output was generic, the spend was high, and the results were mediocre. They chose us — a founder-led team — over established names. And they came back. Through COVID. Through two wedding seasons. Through the kind of conditions that usually break vendor relationships.

What We Did
01

Tight audience targeting

Sharp segmentation built around restaurant patrons and wedding planners — no broad spray.

02

Use-case-specific creative

Restaurant creative for restaurant promotions. Wedding creative for wedding leads. No generic hospitality stock.

03

Continuous testing

Daily creative angles, ad formats and audience overlays — rotated by performance, not by hunch.

04

No agency theatre

Modest budgets, daily monitoring, weekly review. Just the work.

The Result
