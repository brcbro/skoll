The Brief

12,500 sq ft microbrewery across two floors. One of NCR's biggest, busiest and most iconic F&B venues. They needed a partner who could think in systems — not just social posts.

What We Did
01

Campaign worlds, not posts

360-degree work spanning digital, print, experiential and in-venue.

02

Custom visual systems

Custom logos, retro illustration art, infographic table mats — every campaign got its own design language.

03

In-venue activations

Entrance arch installations. Themed table mats. The space itself became part of the campaign.

04

Spirits partnerships

Partnership events with Patron and Jameson — bringing the brands into the venue, not just on the menu.

What It Produced

Best Oktoberfest

In venue history. Themed weeks, branded steins, custom F&B menu.

Tacos & Tequila Fiestas

Cross-cuisine takeover weekends. Sombrero-themed visual language.

Sombrero Sunday Brunches

Recurring Sunday programming — became a signature Gurgaon weekend ritual.

Retro Nation DJ Nights

Vintage poster art, retro music programming, a full visual world.

A venue doesn't become iconic by accident. It becomes iconic when its campaigns are treated like products.
