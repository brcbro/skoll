How We Work

Four phases. One compounding system.

This isn't a process diagram for a pitch deck. It's the actual way we work, in order, every time. The phases compound — skip one and the whole system collapses.

Phase 01

Diagnose

Listen before you prescribe.

Most agencies pitch solutions in the first meeting. We don't. The first 2-4 weeks of any engagement are diagnostic — an audit of the business, market, audience, channels, funnel, creative, analytics, team and current systems.

What this phase produces

  • A complete picture of where you are
  • The real bottlenecks (not the symptoms)
  • Honest assessment of what's working
  • Priorities ranked by impact, not effort
  • A go/no-go on whether we're the right partner
Phase 02

Architect

Design the system. Then design the work.

Once the diagnosis is done, we architect. Not a campaign — a system. We decide which pillars apply, in what order, with what budget and against what metric.

What this phase produces

  • A growth architecture document (the blueprint)
  • Creative direction and brand voice frameworks
  • Channel strategy and media mix
  • KPI tree and measurement plan
  • Roadmap and milestone timeline
Phase 03

Build

Execute with precision.

This is where most agencies start. For us, it's the third phase. By the time we're building, we've earned the right to. The diagnosis told us why. The architecture told us what. Now we build.

What this phase produces

  • Live campaigns and content
  • Functional product (websites, apps, platforms)
  • Performance infrastructure (ad accounts, tracking, dashboards)
  • Brand assets and creative libraries
  • Operational playbooks for your team
Phase 04

Compound

Growth isn't a spike. It's a system that gets better every month.

This is the phase most agencies don't have. They build, they hand off, and they move on. We compound. Daily monitoring, weekly optimisation, monthly review, quarterly strategy.

What this phase produces

  • Compounding performance gains
  • Audience pools that get warmer over time
  • Creative libraries that get smarter with data
  • Reduced CAC and improved efficiency
  • A business that grows on its own gravity
