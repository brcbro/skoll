The Brief

Lakme Academy needed lead generation across 10 cities in North India. They had their own internal marketing team. We were brought in to outperform them.

What We Did
01

Systematic testing

Across 10 city campaigns — no shortcuts, no assumed audiences.

02

City-level refinement

Audience tuning per geography. What worked in Delhi was rewritten for Lucknow.

03

Creative rotation by performance

If it didn't convert, it didn't run. Simple.

04

Compounding discipline

Month over month, the same operating cadence. No flash. No hero campaign.

The Result
Total spend: Rs.1.82 Lakh (across 2 years)
Total conversions: 8,670
Cost per conversion: Rs.21
Cities covered: 10
Outcome: Outperformed Lakme HQ's own lead generation team
No flash. No hero campaign. Just disciplined, compounding work — month over month — that quietly outperformed an in-house team across ten cities.
