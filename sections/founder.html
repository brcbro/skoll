The Founder

No degree.
No network.
Just the work.

Harsh Bhagwani founded Skoll Digital after a decade of marketing work across FMCG, hospitality, beauty, e-commerce and entertainment. He had no marketing background going in. Everything since has been built from scratch.

"I had no marketing background — no degree, no family in the industry, no network. I started by writing Bollywood content for a digital magazine. Everything since then has been built from scratch."

Harsh Bhagwani, Founder

Chapter 01

How it started.

Harsh started in a world far from strategy decks and ad dashboards: writing Bollywood content for a digital magazine. The editor, short-staffed, asked him to also manage the magazine's social media. Something clicked.

Marketing was a thinking discipline — and one Harsh found he was unreasonably good at.

Chapter 02

The break: 7 Degrees & NEU Salonz.

At BrandHype in Gurgaon, Harsh acquired two clients that would shape his career: 7 Degrees Brauhaus, NCR's hottest microbrewery, and NEU Salonz, a six-branch premium salon group.

The work for 7 Degrees became category-defining — Oktoberfest campaigns and branded experiential events. The work for NEU was bigger. A Valentine's Day campaign that worked so well NEU poached him as their Marketing Head.

Chapter 03

Two years in-house at NEU.

For two years, Harsh ran marketing at NEU full-time. The campaigns from that period became local legends:

  • Game of Tones

    Hair-colour campaign timed to a Game of Thrones season launch. Themed F&B (DothraTEA, HODORITOS), GOT soundtrack in-salon, an iron throne photo cutout and a Joffrey dartboard. Sold a year’s worth of hair colour in roughly two months. Nykaa later copied the campaign name.

  • South Point Mall 6th Anniversary

    Member-exclusive event. Grand decor, premium F&B, curated goodies, a 7-foot selfie wall and hired photographers. ₹21 Lakh in sales in 3 days.

  • imNEU Membership

    Repositioned the salon membership as a lifestyle membership. PVC cards in White, Silver, Gold and Black tiers with cross-brand partnerships. 2x memberships in 30 days.

  • NEUresolution

    Annual purpose campaign. Makeovers for underprivileged children and homeless transformation films. 500K+ views on Facebook. Positioned NEU as a brand with a soul.

Chapter 04

The reputation that followed.

After NEU, every major salon brand in Gurgaon came calling: Looks Salon, Geetanjali, Marco Aldany, Headmasters, Nikhil Sharma Hairdressing and Dessange Paris. Harsh didn't pitch any of them. They came to him.

The work also broadened to global giants: Gillette, Tinder, Pee Safe, Jindal Panther, Pullman and Novotel.

Chapter 05

Today, Skoll works with brands across nine industries — from Hyderabad and Gurgaon to Dubai and the United States.
